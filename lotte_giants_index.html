Lotte Giants is a proffesional baseball team in KBO League, based in Busan, South Korea. Founded in 1982, it is one of the longest existing team in the league. Despite its long history, it was almost never a strong team, with only two championships in 1984 and 1992. Still, there are many players that represent the team, Choi Dongwon to name one. Also, it is arguably the most famous team in the league. The city Busan is very well known for baseball that it even has a nickname "Gudo Busan", meaning the city of baseball in Korean.
