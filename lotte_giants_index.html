Home Page

Often, people Busan the city of baseball. Of course, it is the city where many great baseball players like Dong-won Choi, Dae-ho Lee, and Shin-soo Choo were born. But in that logic, Gwangju, Gongju, and Incheon should also be the city of baseball, not to mention Seoul with the most baseball teams. Neither, Lotte Giants based in Busan is not considered to be the most prestigious team. In fact, it has not won the Korean series for 20 years, the longest in the league except three teams that joined the league in the 21st century. It is also the team that has a disgraceful record of being at the bottom for four years in a row (2001-2004)

Nevertheless, citizens of Busan and South Gyeongsang Province have unusual love for Lotte. In fact, Lotte is the only team to surpass the 1 million spectators mark in home games for the past five years in a row. Lotte also holds the record of leargest spectators for a single season of the league, in 2009 with 1.38 million people. What's the secret for the Lotte Giants to have been absolutely loved by baseball fans in Busan for more than 30 years? Let's find out.
